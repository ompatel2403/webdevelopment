When did coffee first appear in the india?

Coffee was introduced to India during the late seventeenth century. The story goes that an Indian pilgrim to Mecca known as Baba Budan smuggled seven beans back to India from Yemen in 1670 (it was illegal to take coffee seeds out of Arabia at the time) and planted them in the Chandragiri hills of Karnataka. (Source: India-Mercanta(The Coffee Hunters))
